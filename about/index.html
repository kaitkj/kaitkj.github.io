About

Hardware Product Development | Cross-Functional Project Leadership | Industrial Design | Mechanical Design | Product Strategy

A great product is purposeful, intentional, reliable, and delightful to use. But building one is rarely straightforward. Product development is a constant balance of technical trade-offs, engineering constraints, business priorities, and cross-functional collaboration. That's where I thrive, bringing together multidisciplinary teams to solve complex engineering challenges and turn ideas into products that people genuinely enjoy using. With four years of experience bringing embedded hardware products from concept to launch, I strive to turn every product development challenge into an opportunity to learn and improve. My work spans project management, industrial design, mechanical design, manufacturing collaboration, and product strategy. I enjoy connecting cross-functional teams to deliver products that are technically sound, thoughtfully designed, and manufacturable at scale. I'm passionate about consumer electronics, IoT devices, AI-enabled hardware, robotics, and building products that create meaningful user experiences. I'm excited to continue learning, building great products, and collaborating with talented people across engineering, design, manufacturing, and business. Always happy to connect with others who share a passion for building great products. (LinkedIn)
